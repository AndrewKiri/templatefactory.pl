Edged board

Unedged board

Unedged board made of 1st or 2nd grade quality pine wood logs. The minimum thickness of the board - 20mm and the length ranges between 2 and 4 meters.

Long strips of squared pine timber with dimensions ranging as follows: 32 × 32 mm up to 90 × 90 mm or 32 × 50 mm up to 75 × 150 mm
